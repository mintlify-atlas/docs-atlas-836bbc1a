## Overview

The user management system allows administrators to manage all platform users including customers, sellers, staff members, and their associated permissions and activities.

## Customer Management

### Viewing Customers

Access the complete list of verified customers.

**Route**: `/admin/customers`

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Customers">
    Go to Users > Customers from the admin menu
    
    **Screenshot Description**: Admin menu with Users dropdown expanded showing Customers option
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Search Customers">
    Use the search bar to find customers by:
    - Name
    - Email address
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View Customer Details">
    Click on a customer to view their complete profile and order history
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Creating Customers

Manually add new customers to the platform.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Customer Creation">
    Click "Add New Customer" button
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Customer Information">
    Fill in required fields:
    - Full name (required)
    - Email address (required, must be unique)
    - Phone number (required, must be unique)
    
    **Route**: POST `/admin/customers/store`
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save Customer">
    Click "Save" to create the customer account
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Customers created through the admin panel are automatically verified and can immediately place orders.
</Note>

### Banning Customers

Restrict customer access when necessary.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Locate Customer">
    Find the customer in the customer list
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Toggle Ban Status">
    Click the ban/unban button
    
    **Route**: `/admin/customers_ban/{customer}`
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm Action">
    - Success message: "Customer Banned Successfully"
    - To unban: "Customer UnBanned Successfully"
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Banned customers cannot:
- Log into their accounts
- Place new orders
- Access their dashboard

Their existing data remains in the system.
</Tip>

### Logging in as Customer

View the platform from a customer's perspective.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Customer">
    From the customer list, locate the customer account
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click Login Button">
    Click "Login as Customer"
    
    **Route**: `/admin/customers/login/{id}`
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Access Customer Dashboard">
    You'll be logged in as that customer and redirected to their dashboard
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
This feature is useful for:
- Troubleshooting customer issues
- Verifying customer experience
- Placing test orders

Always log out and return to your admin account after testing.
</Note>

### Deleting Customers

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Customer(s)">
    Choose customers to delete from the list
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Execute Delete">
    - Single delete: Click delete icon for individual customer
    - Bulk delete: Select multiple and use bulk action
    
    **Routes**:
    - Single: `/admin/customers/destroy/{id}`
    - Bulk: POST `/admin/bulk-customer-delete`
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm Deletion">
    Confirm to permanently remove customer data
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Seller Management

### Viewing Sellers

Access all registered sellers on the platform.

**Route**: `/admin/sellers`

### Seller Verification

Review and approve seller verification requests.

<Steps>
  <Step title="View Verification Requests">
    Navigate to Sellers > Verification Requests
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review Submitted Documents">
    Click on a pending request to view:
    - Business information
    - Verification documents
    - Submitted form data
    
    **Route**: `/admin/sellers/view/{id}/verification`
    
    **Screenshot Description**: Seller verification form showing uploaded documents and business details
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Approve or Reject">
    - To approve: Click "Approve Seller"
      **Route**: `/admin/sellers/approve/{id}`
    
    - To reject: Click "Reject Request"
      **Route**: `/admin/sellers/reject/{id}`
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Managing Seller Status

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Seller Profile">
    Click on a seller from the seller list
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Update Approval Status">
    Toggle seller approval status to enable/disable their ability to sell
    
    **Route**: POST `/admin/sellers/approved`
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Ban/Unban Seller">
    Restrict seller access if needed
    
    **Route**: `/admin/sellers_ban/{id}`
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Seller Payment History

Track payments made to sellers.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Payments">
    Go to Sellers > Payment History
    
    **Route**: `/admin/seller/payments`
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View Payment Details">
    Click on a payment to see full transaction details
    
    **Route**: `/admin/seller/payments/show/{id}`
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Seller Withdraw Requests

Manage seller withdrawal requests.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Withdraw Requests">
    Navigate to Sellers > Withdraw Requests
    
    **Route**: `/admin/withdraw_requests_all`
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review Request">
    Click on a request to view:
    - Requested amount
    - Seller's available balance
    - Payment method preferences
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Process Payment">
    Open the payment modal and process the withdrawal
    
    **Route**: POST `/admin/withdraw_request/payment_modal`
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Logging in as Seller

Access the seller panel as a specific seller.

**Route**: `/admin/sellers/login/{id}`

<Tip>
Use this to:
- Help sellers with technical issues
- Verify seller panel functionality
- Train new sellers
</Tip>

### Bulk Seller Operations

Delete multiple sellers simultaneously.

**Route**: POST `/admin/bulk-seller-delete`

## Staff Management

### Creating Staff Accounts

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Staff Section">
    Navigate to Users > Staff
    
    **Route**: `/admin/staffs`
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add New Staff">
    Click "Add New Staff Member"
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Assign Role">
    Select a role with appropriate permissions
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Staff Details">
    Fill in name, email, and password
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save Staff Account">
    Create the account with assigned permissions
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Role Management

Define roles and permissions for staff members.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Roles">
    Go to Users > Roles
    
    **Route**: `/admin/roles`
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Create New Role">
    Click "Add New Role" and enter role name
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Assign Permissions">
    Select permissions for this role from available options:
    - View all customers
    - Login as customer
    - Ban customer
    - Delete customer
    - Seller commission configuration
    - Product management
    - Order management
    - And many more...
    
    **Route**: POST `/admin/roles/add_permission`
    
    **Screenshot Description**: Permission assignment interface showing categorized permission checkboxes
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save Role">
    Save the role with selected permissions
  </Step>
</Steps>

## User Permissions

Key permission categories:

### Customer Permissions
- `view_all_customers`: View customer list
- `login_as_customer`: Access customer accounts
- `ban_customer`: Ban/unban customers
- `delete_customer`: Remove customer accounts

### Seller Permissions
- `seller_commission_configuration`: Manage commission rates
- `seller_verification_form_configuration`: Configure verification requirements
- `pay_to_affiliate_user`: Process affiliate payments
- `view_affiliate_users`: Access affiliate user list

### General Settings Permissions
- `general_settings`: Access general configuration
- `features_activation`: Enable/disable platform features
- `smtp_settings`: Configure email settings
- `payment_methods_configurations`: Manage payment gateways
- `order_configuration`: Configure order settings

## Best Practices

1. **Regular Audits**: Review user accounts regularly
2. **Permission Management**: Grant minimum necessary permissions to staff
3. **Verification**: Always verify seller documents thoroughly
4. **Customer Support**: Use "login as customer" to troubleshoot issues
5. **Secure Deletion**: Backup data before bulk deletions
