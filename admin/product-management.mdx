## Overview

The product management system allows administrators to oversee all products on the platform, including those created by sellers and in-house products. You can manage product approval, featured status, and inventory settings.

## Product Types

### Admin Products

Products created and managed directly by the platform administration.

**Route**: `/admin/products/admin`

### Seller Products

Products uploaded by individual sellers that may require approval.

**Route**: `/admin/products/seller/{product_type}`

Product types include:
- Physical products
- Digital products
- Wholesale products
- Auction products

### All Products View

Access a comprehensive list of all products across the platform.

**Route**: `/admin/products/all`

## Managing Products

### Creating Products

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Product Creation">
    Go to Products > Create New Product
    
    **Screenshot Description**: Product menu with "Create New Product" button highlighted
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Fill Product Information">
    Enter the following details:
    - Product name
    - Category selection
    - Brand (optional)
    - Unit and description
    - Tags for searchability
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set Pricing and Stock">
    Configure:
    - Unit price
    - SKU combinations (if variants exist)
    - Current stock levels
    - Color options (if applicable)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add VAT & Tax">
    Select applicable tax rates and types:
    - Tax ID
    - Tax percentage
    - Tax type (inclusive/exclusive)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save Product">
    Click "Save" to create the product
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Editing Products

<Steps>
  <Step title="Locate Product">
    Find the product using the search function or browse product listings
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Access Edit Mode">
    Click the edit icon next to the product
    
    **Admin products**: `/admin/products/admin/{id}/edit`  
    **Seller products**: `/admin/products/seller/{id}/edit`
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Modify Product Details">
    Update any product information, stock levels, or configurations
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Update Product">
    Save changes to update the product
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Product Features

### Featured Products

Mark products as featured to highlight them on the homepage.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Products">
    Choose products from the product list
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Toggle Featured Status">
    Use the featured toggle switch or bulk action menu
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm Changes">
    Featured products will appear in designated homepage sections
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Today's Deal

Promote products in the "Today's Deal" section for increased visibility.

**Route**: POST `/admin/products/todays_deal`

### Product Approval

If seller product approval is enabled, review and approve seller products.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review Pending Products">
    Navigate to Seller Products with "Pending" status filter
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Evaluate Product">
    Check product details, images, pricing, and descriptions
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Approve or Reject">
    Toggle the approval status
    
    **Route**: POST `/admin/products/approved`
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Product approval is controlled by the `product_approve_by_admin` business setting. When enabled, all seller products require admin approval before appearing on the storefront.
</Note>

## Category Management

### Creating Categories

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Categories">
    Navigate to Categories section from the admin menu
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add New Category">
    Click "Add New Category" button
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Category Details">
    - Category name
    - Parent category (for subcategories)
    - Level (0 for root categories)
    - Category type (physical/digital)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set Featured Status">
    Mark categories as featured for homepage display
    
    **Route**: POST `/admin/categories/featured`
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Category Types

**Route**: POST `/admin/categories/categoriesByType`

Filter categories by:
- Physical products (`digital = 0`)
- Digital products (`digital = 1`)

## Brand Management

Manage product brands across the platform.

**Routes**:
- List brands: `/admin/brands`
- Create: `/admin/brands/create`
- Edit: `/admin/brands/edit/{id}`
- Delete: `/admin/brands/destroy/{id}`

## Bulk Operations

### Bulk Product Upload

Upload multiple products via CSV.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download Template">
    Get the CSV template: `/admin/product-csv-download/{type}`
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Fill Product Data">
    Complete the CSV with product information
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Upload CSV">
    Use the bulk upload interface at `/admin/product-bulk-upload/index`
    
    **Route**: POST `/admin/bulk-product-upload`
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Bulk Product Delete

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Products">
    Check multiple products in the product list
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Execute Bulk Delete">
    Click "Delete Selected" button
    
    **Route**: POST `/admin/bulk-product-delete`
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm Deletion">
    Confirm the action to permanently delete products
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Download reference PDFs for bulk uploads:
- Categories: `/admin/bulk-upload/download/category`
- Brands: `/admin/bulk-upload/download/brand`
- Sellers: `/admin/bulk-upload/download/seller`
</Tip>

## Product Attributes

### Managing Attributes

Create and manage product attributes (size, color, material, etc.).

**Routes**:
- List: `/admin/attributes`
- Create: `/admin/attributes/create`
- Edit: `/admin/attributes/edit/{id}`
- Store attribute values: POST `/admin/store-attribute-value`

### Color Management

Manage color options for products.

**Routes**:
- View colors: `/admin/colors`
- Add color: POST `/admin/colors/store`
- Edit: `/admin/colors/edit/{id}`
- Update: POST `/admin/colors/update/{id}`

## Digital Products

### Managing Digital Products

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Digital Products">
    Navigate to Digital Products section
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Upload Digital File">
    Attach the downloadable file to the product
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set Download Limits">
    Configure download restrictions (if applicable)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Download Product File">
    Admins can download digital product files: `/admin/digitalproducts/download/{id}`
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Product Duplication

Quickly create similar products by duplicating existing ones.

**Route**: `/admin/products/duplicate/{id}`

<Note>
Duplication copies all product details, stock configurations, and tax settings to the new product.
</Note>
