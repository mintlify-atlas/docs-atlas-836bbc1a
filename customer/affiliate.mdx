## Overview

The affiliate program allows you to earn commissions by referring new customers and sharing products. This guide covers joining the program, generating referrals, and earning commissions.

<Note>
The affiliate system must be activated by the platform administrator. Check if the program is available on your platform.
</Note>

## Affiliate Program Benefits

- **Earn Commissions**: Get paid for successful referrals
- **Multiple Commission Types**: Earn from customer registrations and product sharing
- **Track Performance**: Monitor clicks, conversions, and earnings
- **Flexible Withdrawals**: Request payouts when you reach minimum threshold
- **No Limits**: Unlimited earning potential

## Joining the Affiliate Program

### Eligibility

To join the affiliate program:

- Must have an active customer account
- Complete the affiliate verification form
- Submit required documentation
- Wait for admin approval

### Application Process

**Route**: `/affiliate/apply`

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Application Form">
    Navigate to the affiliate section or click "Become an Affiliate"
    
    **Screenshot Description**: Affiliate application page with registration form
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Login or Register">
    If not logged in:
    - Create a customer account first
    - Or login to existing account
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Complete Verification Form">
    Fill out required information based on platform requirements:
    - **Text Fields**: Business information, address, contact details
    - **Select Fields**: Business type, marketing methods
    - **Multi-select**: Social media platforms you'll use
    - **File Uploads**: ID verification, business documents
    
    **Route**: POST `/affiliate/store`
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Submit Application">
    Click "Submit Application"
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Await Approval">
    - Admin receives notification of your application
    - You receive notification when approved/rejected
    - Approval typically takes 1-3 business days
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
If you already have a customer account, the affiliate application links to your existing account. If registering fresh, you'll create login credentials during the application.
</Note>

## Understanding Commission Types

The platform may offer different affiliate commission structures:

### 1. User Registration First Purchase

Earn commission when someone you refer makes their first purchase.

**How it works:**
- Share your unique referral link
- New user registers using your link
- User makes their first purchase
- You earn a percentage of that order total

**Commission calculation:**
```
Commission = (Percentage × Order Total) / 100
```

<Tip>
This commission type rewards you for bringing new customers to the platform. Focus on reaching potential new users.
</Tip>

### 2. Product Sharing

Earn commission when someone purchases a product you shared.

**How it works:**
- Generate a product-specific referral link
- Share the link (social media, blog, email, etc.)
- Customer clicks your link and purchases
- You earn commission on that product sale

**Commission types:**
- **Fixed Amount**: Earn a set amount per sale
- **Percentage**: Earn percentage of product price

<Note>
Only one product sharing method is active at a time (either product sharing or category-wise affiliate).
</Note>

### 3. Category-Wise Affiliate

Earn different commission rates based on product category.

**How it works:**
- Different categories have different commission rates
- Share products from high-commission categories
- Earn based on category-specific rates

**Commission structure:**
- Each category has its own rate
- Can be fixed amount or percentage
- Automatically calculated based on product category

## Earning Commissions

### When Commissions Are Credited

Commissions are added to your balance when:

1. **Order is Placed**: Tracked in system
2. **Payment is Confirmed**: Order marked as "Paid"
3. **Commission Calculated**: Automatic calculation
4. **Balance Updated**: Added to your affiliate balance

### Commission Validation

If validation time is configured:

- Commissions may have a holding period
- Ensures orders aren't cancelled or returned
- Balance becomes available after validation time
- Protects against fraudulent referrals

## Withdrawing Earnings

### Payment Settings

**Route**: `/affiliate/user/payment-settings`

<Steps>
  <Step title="Configure Payment Methods">
    Navigate to Affiliate > Payment Settings
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add Payment Information">
    Enter details for:
    - **PayPal**: PayPal email address
    - **Bank Transfer**: Account number, routing number, bank name
    - Other available methods
    
    **Screenshot Description**: Payment settings page with PayPal and bank information fields
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save Settings">
    **Route**: POST `/affiliate/user/payment-settings/store`
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Requesting Withdrawal

**Route**: `/affiliate/user/withdraw-request`

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Available Balance">
    Ensure you meet minimum withdrawal amount
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Access Withdrawal Page">
    Navigate to Affiliate > Withdraw
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Amount">
    Specify how much you want to withdraw
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Submit Request">
    **Route**: POST `/affiliate/user/withdraw-request/store`
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Automatic Deduction">
    - Amount is deducted from your balance immediately
    - Prevents duplicate withdrawal requests
    - Request status: Pending (`status = 0`)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Admin Processing">
    Admin reviews and processes your request
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Receive Payment">
    Funds transferred via your chosen payment method
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Withdrawal Request Statuses

- **Pending (0)**: Awaiting admin approval
- **Approved (1)**: Payment processed
- **Rejected (2)**: Request declined (amount returned to balance)

### Withdrawal History

**Route**: `/affiliate/user/withdraw-request-history`

<Steps>
  <Step title="View All Requests">
    Navigate to Affiliate > Withdrawal History
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Check Request Status">
    Monitor pending and completed withdrawals
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review Payments">
    See dates and amounts of past payouts
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
If a withdrawal request is rejected:
- Amount is credited back to your balance
- You can submit a new request
- Check rejection reason from admin
</Note>

## Best Practices for Affiliates

### Content Creation

1. **Honest Reviews**: Write genuine product reviews
2. **Quality Content**: Create valuable content for your audience
3. **Clear Disclosure**: Disclose affiliate relationships
4. **Visual Content**: Use images and videos
5. **SEO Optimization**: Optimize content for search engines

### Marketing Strategies

1. **Social Media**: Share on Facebook, Instagram, Twitter
2. **Email Marketing**: Build email list and send newsletters
3. **Blogging**: Write product reviews and comparisons
4. **YouTube**: Create unboxing and review videos
5. **Pinterest**: Pin products with affiliate links
6. **Forums**: Participate in relevant communities

### Choosing Products

1. **Know Your Audience**: Promote products they need
2. **High-Quality Items**: Only recommend quality products
3. **Good Commission**: Focus on products with better rates
4. **Seasonal Trends**: Leverage seasonal demand
5. **Personal Experience**: Promote products you've used

### Compliance

1. **Follow Guidelines**: Adhere to platform rules
2. **No Spam**: Don't send unsolicited messages
3. **Honest Marketing**: No false claims or misleading info
4. **Respect Privacy**: Don't share customer information
5. **Disclose Relationships**: Be transparent about affiliate links

## Affiliate Limitations

Some platforms may impose:

- **Maximum Affiliate Limit**: Cap on referral earnings per period
- **Validation Time**: Holding period for commissions
- **Minimum Withdrawal**: Minimum amount for payout
- **Inactive Accounts**: Removal of inactive affiliates

<Tip>
Check your platform's specific affiliate terms and conditions for detailed rules and limitations.
</Tip>

## Troubleshooting

**Q: My referral link isn't working**  
A:
- Verify your affiliate status is approved
- Check if you copied the complete link
- Ensure affiliate system is active
- Test link in incognito/private browsing

**Q: Commission wasn't credited**  
A:
- Check if order was paid (commission only on paid orders)
- Verify order wasn't cancelled
- Check validation time requirements
- Review affiliate logs for the referral

**Q: Withdrawal request was rejected**  
A:
- Check admin's rejection message
- Verify minimum withdrawal amount
- Ensure payment details are complete
- Contact support for clarification

**Q: Can't see affiliate dashboard**  
A:
- Confirm your affiliate application was approved
- Check if you're logged in
- Verify affiliate system is enabled
- Contact admin if approved but no access

**Q: Different commission than expected**  
A:
- Check which commission type is active
- Review category-specific rates
- Verify calculation method (fixed vs percentage)
- Contact support with specific examples
