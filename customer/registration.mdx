## Overview

Creating a customer account on Ecom gives you access to personalized shopping features, order tracking, wishlists, wallet functionality, and more. This guide walks you through the registration process.

## Registration Methods

### Standard Email Registration

Create an account using your email address:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Registration Page">
    Click "Sign Up" or "Register" on the homepage
    
    **Screenshot Description**: Homepage header with registration button highlighted
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Your Information">
    Fill in the registration form:
    - **Full Name**: Your complete name
    - **Email Address**: Valid email (must be unique)
    - **Phone Number**: Contact number (must be unique)
    - **Password**: Strong password (minimum requirements apply)
    - **Confirm Password**: Re-enter your password
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Accept Terms and Conditions">
    Read and accept the platform's terms of service and privacy policy
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Complete Registration">
    Click "Register" or "Create Account" to submit
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Email Verification

If email verification is enabled:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Your Email">
    After registration, check your inbox for a verification email
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click Verification Link">
    Open the email and click the verification link
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm Verification">
    Your email is now verified and your account is fully activated
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Start Shopping">
    Log in and begin browsing products
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
If email verification is disabled, your account is immediately activated upon registration.
</Note>

## Account Validation

### Email Uniqueness

- Each email address can only be registered once
- If your email is already in use, you'll see: "Email already exists!"
- Use the "Forgot Password" feature if you've previously registered

### Phone Number Uniqueness

- Each phone number can only be registered once
- Ensure you enter the correct phone number
- Phone numbers are used for order notifications

### Password Requirements

Create a strong password:
- Minimum length (typically 8 characters)
- Mix of uppercase and lowercase letters
- Include numbers
- Special characters recommended
- Must match confirmation field

<Note>
Password requirements may vary based on platform configuration. The registration form will display specific requirements.
</Note>

## After Registration

### Automatic Account Setup

When you register, the system automatically:

1. **Creates User Account**: Your main login credentials
2. **Creates Customer Profile**: Links to your user account
3. **Generates Referral Code**: Unique code for referring others (if affiliate system is active)
4. **Sets User Type**: Marks you as "customer"
5. **Initializes Wallet**: Creates wallet account (if wallet system is active)

## Troubleshooting Registration

### Common Issues

**Q: "Email already exists" error**  
A: This email is already registered. Use "Forgot Password" to recover your account or register with a different email.

**Q: "Phone already exists" error**  
A: This phone number is registered to another account. Use a different number or contact support.

**Q: Didn't receive verification email**  
A: 
1. Check spam/junk folder
2. Ensure email address is correct
3. Wait a few minutes and check again
4. Request a new verification email
5. Contact support if issue persists

**Q: Social login isn't working**  
A:
1. Ensure social login is enabled on the platform
2. Check your social media account is active
3. Try logging in directly with email/password
4. Contact support for assistance

**Q: Password doesn't meet requirements**  
A: Check the specific requirements displayed on the form and ensure your password includes:
- Minimum length
- Mixed case letters
- Numbers
- Special characters (if required)
