## Overview

Your shop is your brand identity on the platform. A well-configured shop attracts customers and builds trust. This guide covers all aspects of shop configuration.

## Accessing Shop Settings

**Route**: `/seller/shop`

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Shop Settings">
    From your seller dashboard, click "Shop Settings" or "My Shop"
    
    **Screenshot Description**: Seller dashboard menu with Shop Settings option highlighted
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View Current Configuration">
    Review your existing shop information
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Edit Shop Details">
    Update any section and save changes
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Basic Shop Information

### Shop Name and Identity

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter Shop Name">
    Choose a unique, memorable name for your shop
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Upload Shop Logo">
    - Recommended size: 300x300px
    - Format: PNG with transparent background preferred
    - Maximum file size: 2MB
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set Meta Information">
    - **Meta Title**: SEO-friendly shop title (50-60 characters)
    - **Meta Description**: Brief shop description for search engines (150-160 characters)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save Basic Information">
    **Route**: POST `/seller/shop/update`
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Your shop slug (URL) is automatically generated from your shop name plus shop ID. Example: `my-shop-123`
</Note>

### Contact Information

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter Business Address">
    Provide your complete business address for customer confidence
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add Phone Number">
    Include a valid contact number for customer support
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Verify Contact Details">
    Ensure all information is accurate before saving
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Shipping Configuration

### Flat Rate Shipping

If you offer flat-rate shipping:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable Shipping Cost">
    Check if shipping cost field is available in your shop settings
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set Shipping Cost">
    Enter your standard shipping charge
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save Shipping Settings">
    Update shop settings with shipping cost
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Consider offering free shipping on orders above a certain amount to encourage larger purchases.
</Tip>

### Pickup Location

If you offer local pickup:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Configure Pickup Location">
    Navigate to the pickup location section
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set Coordinates">
    Enter latitude and longitude for your pickup location:
    - `delivery_pickup_latitude`
    - `delivery_pickup_longitude`
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save Pickup Settings">
    **Route**: POST `/seller/shop/update`
  </Step>
</Steps>

Connect your social media profiles to build trust and increase reach.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Social Media Section">
    Scroll to the social media links section in shop settings
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add Social Profile URLs">

## Shop Banners and Branding

### Banner Configuration

Customize your shop page with promotional banners.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Upload Top Banner">
    - Recommended size: 1920x400px
    - Use for main promotions or branding
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add Slider Images">
    Upload multiple images for a rotating slider:
    - Recommended size: 1920x500px
    - Maximum 5 images recommended
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Configure Full-Width Banner 1">
    Add a full-width promotional banner
    - Size: 1920x300px
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add Half-Width Banners">
    Upload two side-by-side banners:
    - Size: 960x300px each
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Configure Full-Width Banner 2">
    Add a second full-width banner at the bottom
    - Size: 1920x300px
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save Banner Configuration">
    **Route**: POST `/seller/shop/update`
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Banner Design Tips:
- Use high-quality images
- Include clear call-to-action text
- Maintain consistent branding
- Update seasonally for promotions
- Optimize file sizes for fast loading
</Tip>

## Shop Verification

Increase customer trust by getting your shop verified.

### Applying for Verification

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Verification Status">
    In your shop settings, check if verification is available
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Access Verification Form">
    Navigate to "Apply for Verification"
    
    **Route**: `/seller/shop/apply-for-verification`
    
    **Screenshot Description**: Verification application form with document upload sections
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Complete Verification Form">
    Fill out all required fields based on the platform's verification requirements:
    - Text fields for business information
    - Select options for business type
    - File uploads for documents
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Upload Required Documents">
    Attach necessary verification documents
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Submit Application">
    **Route**: POST `/seller/shop/verification_info_store`
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Await Admin Review">
    You and the admin will receive notifications about the submission
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
You can only submit one verification request at a time. If rejected, your verification info will be cleared and you can reapply.
</Note>

## Shop URL and SEO

### Shop Slug

Your shop URL is automatically generated:
- Format: `{shop-name}-{shop-id}`
- Example: `best-electronics-42`
- Updates automatically when you change shop name

### SEO Optimization

<Steps>
  <Step title="Optimize Meta Title">
    Create a descriptive, keyword-rich title:
    - Include your shop name
    - Add primary product category
    - Keep under 60 characters
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Write Meta Description">
    Craft a compelling description:
    - Summarize what you sell
    - Include key benefits
    - Add location if relevant
    - Stay within 160 characters
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Q: My shop slug looks strange**  
A: The slug is auto-generated from your shop name. Choose a clean shop name without special characters.

**Q: Banner images aren't displaying correctly**  
A: Ensure images meet the recommended dimensions and are in JPG or PNG format.

**Q: I can't find the verification option**  
A: Verification may be disabled or you may have already submitted a request.

**Q: Changes aren't saving**  
A: Check that all required fields are filled and try clearing your browser cache.
